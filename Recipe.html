Chicken N' Waffles

By Adebola Falade

I made this breakfast because it's a perfect combo if you want something sweet & meaty, without eating chinese. So this's a tasty nutritious dish!.

INGREDIENTS

  • Fried Chicken:
  • chicken thighs
  • buttermilk
  • all-purpose flour
  • seasoned salt
  • paprika
  • black pepper
  • dried thyme
  • cayenne pepper
  • milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • cup sugar
  • baking powder
  • kosher salt
  • vanilla extract
  • eggs
  • salted butter
  • jalapenos

DIRECTIONS

    Fried Chicken:

  1. 8 boneless, skin-on chicken thighs
  2. 2 cups buttermilk
  3. 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, plus more if needed
  4. 1 tablespoon seasoned salt, such as Lawry's
  5. 1 teaspoon paprika
  6. 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  7. 1 teaspoon ground dried thyme
  8. 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper, plus more if needed
  9. 1/4 cup milk, plus more if needed
  10. Vegetable oil, for frying

    11. Waffles:

  11. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  12. 1/4 cup sugar
  13. 1 tablespoon baking powder
  14. 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  15. 1 1/2 cups milk
  16. 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  17. 4 large egg whites plus 2 large egg yolks
  18. 8 tablespoons (1 stick) salted butter, melted
  19. 1 to 2 jalapenos, chopped
